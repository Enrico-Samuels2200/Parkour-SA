Parkour

If you don't already know what parkour is, allow me to
inform you. Parkour is a dicipline that allows yourself to move
effectively from point A to point B. With this you will gain the
knowledge and skill to over come many obstacles with ease. Is that wall
blocking your path? Why not scale over it, instead of going around it.

Why learn Parkour?

Parkour is a great sport to learn. It keeps the body and mind active and
it's also fun. It can be applyed to real life situations. You might miss
that 06:30am bus not anymore. You going to be late for work, because your taxi
is stucked in traffic a few blocks from your workplace, think again. Parkour can
be used in many situations. It could be use it to escape life threatning
situations. Which we hope no one will ever be in that situation.
