Parkour

If you don't already know what parkour is, allow me to
inform you. Parkour is a dicipline that allows yourself to move
effectively from point A to point B. With this you will gain the
knowledge and skill to over come many obstacles with ease. Is that wall
blocking your path? Why not scale over it, instead of going around it.

Why learn Parkour?

Parkour is a great sport to learn. It keeps the body and mind active and
it's also fun. It can be applyed to real life situations. You going to miss
that 06:30am bus not anymore. You going to be late for work, because your public transport
is stucked in traffic a few blocks from your workplace, think again. Parkour can
be used in many situations. It could be use it to escape life threatning
situations. Which we hope no one will ever be in that situation.

Benefits of Parkour


  • It is a full body workout
  • Improved cardiovascular endurance
  • Increased bone strength
  • Promotes quicker thinking
  • Fosters creativity
  • Boosts confidence
  • Skill-related fitness routine
  • Builds core strength

About Us

We are a collective of like minded people, who wish to make a difference
in South Africa. We wish to have parkour recognised as an offical sport in South Africa.
By introducing youth to this sport we will keep them active
in a fun manor in which they enjoy. It's also a way to relief stress, and learn
dicipline. Our ultimate goal is to help prevent the youth of making choices that
lead to crime. We know we can make a difference if they are kept active and are amongst
peers that will encourage them to do better. We want to create a community, a safe place
where the youth can support one another, help each other to become better than they were.
To become the ultimate person they can truly become.
